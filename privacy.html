Privacy Policy – Citizenship Coach

Last updated: November 2025

Citizenship Coach ("the App") is an educational mobile application designed to help users prepare for the U.S. Naturalization Civics Test. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the App.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not collect, store, or share any personal information. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Names or contact details
  • Email addresses or phone numbers
  • Location information
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage or analytics data
  • Payment or financial information

2. Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics tools, advertising networks, or external services that collect user data.

3. Accounts and Logins

The App does not require you to create an account or sign in. You can use all features without providing any personal information.

4. Data Storage and Security

The App does not transmit or store personal data on our servers. All usage is limited to your device. Since we do not collect data, there is no personal information for us to secure or delete.

5. Children’s Privacy

The App is suitable for users of all ages, including children under 13, because it does not collect any personal information.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
